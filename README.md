## Project Overview
<img width="975" height="548" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8d1f6bd8-ac92-4cb7-b7d1-54a27485795a" />


This project presents an end-to-end sales and logistics analysis for **Ferns and Petals (FNP)**, a leading Indian gifting company known for occasion-based products such as Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and Anniversaries. The analysis was performed **entirely in Microsoft Excel**, demonstrating in-depth proficiency in Excel for data analytics.

The goal was to uncover revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency, and translate these insights into actionable business recommendations.

## Dataset Summary
<img width="256" height="165" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/39c695d3-4a29-41f6-91d5-0a5f64ce17fd" />

* **Data Source:** Ferns and Petals sales data (India)
* **Structure:** 3 relational tables (Orders, Customers, Products)
* **Records:** 1,000 Orders | 100 Customers | 70 Products
* **Key Attributes:**

  * Customer demographics
  * Order and delivery timelines
  * Product details and pricing
  * Occasion-based sales

## Data Ingestion & Preparation (Excel + Power Query)

All data processing was handled within Excel:

* Imported multiple source tables using **Power Query**
* Cleaned and validated data (no missing values)
* Engineered new features, including order month, day, hour, and delivery lead time
* Calculated **Total Revenue** using price and quantity

##  Data Analysis

Using **Pivot Tables and calculated measures**, the following analyses were performed:

* Total Revenue and Average Order Value
* Monthly and seasonal sales trends
* Top-performing products and categories
* Occasion-based revenue analysis
* City-wise order distribution
* Delivery time and logistics efficiency
* Correlation analysis between order quantity and delivery time

---

##  Dashboard & Visualization

An **interactive Excel dashboard** was created to communicate insights clearly to stakeholders. It includes:

* KPI cards (Revenue, AOV, Delivery Time)
* Trend and comparison charts
* Filters for dynamic exploration

This demonstrates strong data storytelling and visualization skills using Excel.

---

##  Key Business Insights

* Peak sales occur in **February and August**
* **Anniversaries** generate the highest revenue
* **Colors and Soft Toys** are top-performing categories
* Strong demand observed in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities
* Average delivery time of ~5.5 days shows room for logistics optimization
